# 🏠 Executive Summary Page

**Dashboard Page 1 of 4**

## Overview
The Executive Summary page displays top-line KPIs and key financial metrics at a glance, with trend indicators and regional performance status.

## Key Components

### KPI Cards (Top Row)
- **💰 Revenue:** $180.5M (↓8.3% Prior Year)
- **📈 EBITDA:** $63.2M (↑0.1% Prior Year)
- **🟢 Net Profit:** $35.7M (↑4.4% Prior Year)
- **💧 Cash Flow:** $20.3M (↑5.2% MoM)
- **🔄 Working Capital:** $7.4M (Stable Position)
- **📊 EBITDA Margin:** 35.0% (↑0.3pp Prior Year)

### Interactive Filters
- **Fiscal Year:** FY 2024
- **Quarters:** Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4 (Multi-select)
- **Regions:** All Regions (North, South, East, West)
- **Products:** All Products (Product A, Product B)

### Visual Analytics
1. **Revenue vs Budget — Monthly Trend**
   - Line + Column Combo Chart
   - Actual Revenue vs Budget Revenue with 3M Rolling Average
   - Shows consistent performance exceeding budget

2. **Revenue by Region**
   - Donut Chart with regional breakdown
   - North: 35%, South: 20%, East: 22%, West: 17%

3. **EBITDA Bridge (FY 2024)**
   - Waterfall Chart showing Revenue → COGS → OpEx → EBITDA
   - Visualizes profit flow from top line to operating earnings

4. **YoY Revenue Growth %**
   - Line Chart tracking month-over-month growth trends
   - Shows growth acceleration from 6% to 11%+ through the year

5. **KPI Status by Region**
   - Status Matrix with color-coded performance flags
   - Revenue, EBITDA, Net Profit, and Cash Flow status indicators
   - Color coding: 🟢 Exceeded | 🟡 On Track | 🟠 At Risk | 🔴 Behind

6. **Rolling 3M Average vs Actual**
   - Line Chart showing both actual revenue and 3-month rolling average
   - Helps identify underlying trends vs monthly volatility
