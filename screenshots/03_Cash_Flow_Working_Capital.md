- Trend: Consistent month-to-month improvement

### Accounts Receivable vs Payable Balance
- **Dual-line Chart**
- Orange: Accounts Receivable ($3.2M → $5.5M)
- Red: Accounts Payable ($1.8M → $3.1M)
- AR growing faster than AP indicates increasing sales
- Both trending upward with business growth

### AR & AP Turnover Ratio (Quarterly)
- **Clustered Column Chart**
- Green: AR Turnover (~30x across all quarters)
- Purple: AP Turnover (~7x across all quarters)
- AR Turnover consistently high = efficient collections
- AP Turnover lower = extended payment terms with suppliers

### DSO vs DPO (Days Outstanding)
- **Dual-line Chart**
- Orange: DSO (Days Sales Outstanding) ~44 days
- Blue: DPO (Days Payable Outstanding) ~37 days
- DSO > DPO indicates cash flow gap of ~7 days
- CCC = 48 days (monitoring flag due to working capital tie-up)
