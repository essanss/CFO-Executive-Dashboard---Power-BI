## Overview
The P&L Deep Dive page provides detailed Profit & Loss analysis with margin trends, quarterly breakdowns, and rolling averages for deeper financial insights.

## Key Components

### Income Statement Matrix
- **Full Year 2024 Analysis** ($M)
- Rows: Revenue, COGS, Gross Profit, Operating Expenses, EBITDA, Depreciation, EBIT, Interest Expense, Tax, Net Profit
- Columns: Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4, FY 2024 Total, vs Prior Year

  - Revenue: $180.5M (↑8.3% vs PY)
  - Gross Profit: $108.3M (Margin: 60.0%)
  - EBITDA: $63.2M (Margin: 35.0%, ↑9.1% vs PY)
  - Net Profit: $35.7M (Margin: 19.8%, ↑6.4% vs PY)

### Margin Trends Analysis
- **Multi-line Chart showing three margins:**
  - **Gross Margin:** ~60% (Gold line) — stable across all months
  - **EBITDA Margin:** ~35% (Green line) — showing consistency
  - **Net Margin:** ~19.8% (Blue line) — steady profitability
- Demonstrates operational efficiency and stable cost structure

### Revenue by Business Unit — Quarterly
- **Stacked Column Chart**
- Product A (Orange) vs Product B (Blue) quarterly progression
- Q1: $38.5M | Q2: $42.4M | Q3: $46.7M | Q4: $53.0M

### Month-over-Month EBITDA Movement
- **Waterfall Chart** tracking monthly EBITDA changes
- Starting from January (~$14.8M) to December (~$20.3M)
- Visualizes month-to-month momentum in operating earnings
- Shows sustained improvement throughout FY 2024

### Rolling 3-Month Averages
- **Dual-line Chart**
- Orange: Revenue 3M Rolling Average ($12M → $21M)
- Green: EBITDA 3M Rolling Average ($4M → $7M)
- Smooths out monthly volatility to show underlying trend
- Confirms upward trajectory in both revenue and profitability
